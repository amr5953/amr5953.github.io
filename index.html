a little about me

Hello, I'm Allee 🌸

I'm a curious soul who believes the best way to grow is to get a little uncomfortable. I help companies bridge the gap between technical products and the market — but my real passion? Exploring the world one country at a time.

In the summer of 2024, I took the leap and spent 2 months solo traveling across Europe with nothing but my laptop and a backpack. A month in Portugal exploring Lisbon, Porto and the Algarve, two weeks in Spain hitting Barcelona, Valencia and Seville, and two weeks living like a local in Florence, Italy. I worked remotely from cafés, stayed in hostels, made friends with strangers who became family, and found a version of myself I didn't know existed.

When I'm not traveling internationally, you'll find me hiking trails at US national parks with my dog Presley (yes, named after Elvis 🎸), hunting down the best local food spots, or planning the next adventure.
