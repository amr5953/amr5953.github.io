a little about me

Hello, I'm Allee 🌸

I'm a curious soul who believes the best way to grow is to get a little uncomfortable. I help companies bridge the gap between technical products and the market — but my real passion? Exploring the world one country at a time.

In 2023, I took the leap and spent 2 months solo traveling across Europe with nothing but my laptop and a backpack. I stayed in Airbnbs and hostels, worked remotely from cafés in Lisbon and piazzas in Italy, made friends with strangers who became family, and found a version of myself I didn't know existed.

When I'm not traveling internationally, you'll find me hiking trails at US national parks with my dog, hunting down the best local food spots, or planning the next adventure.
