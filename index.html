About Me

A little bit about the person behind the adventures

Hello, I'm Allee 🌸

I'm a Technical GTM Consultant with a passion for solving complex problems and helping businesses scale. But beyond the work, I'm a curious soul who believes the best way to grow is to get a little uncomfortable.

In 2023, I packed my laptop and spent 2 months solo traveling across Europe — working remotely while staying in Airbnbs and hostels, meeting strangers who became lifelong friends, and learning more about myself than any book could teach.

When I'm not working or traveling, you'll find me on a hiking trail, hunting down the best local restaurant in whatever city I'm in, or spending quality time with my dog. Life is short — I plan to see all of it.
